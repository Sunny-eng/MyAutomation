Our first newsletter shares a handpicked case study for Vodafone, India. It showcases how a simple SMS based mobile app can evoke interest, engage and acquire customers that are otherwise difficult to reach via traditional media.
 
     
Vodafone in India is a key telecom player and has over 106.34 million customers nationwide. The importance of prepaid services in competitive differentiation and revenue generation became apparent as the Indian market became more saturated and the competition intensified. The marketing team initiated a unique 2 pronged promotion to acquire new customers & increase ARPU (Average revenue per user) of the existing prepaid customers through SMS GupShup’s Media Services.
 
 
  Solution  
 
The slums of Mumbai were approached for this promotion since majority of subscribers use prepaid connections. A customized mobile app was developed by SMS GupShup to conduct auctions on SMS/ Text platform using a toll free number.

Essential commodities like Rice, Wheat and Sugar were up for bids. Vodafone prepaid subscribers with a balance of over Rs. 20 were eligible for these products using their mobile phone. The winning bids were announced on the spot.		  
   
  Solution  
 
Over 300 distinct users participated across 52 bidding events with a staggering total of 5092 bids through SMS. The promotion was a huge success and Vodafone has seen migration of subscribers from other networks to its network. The minimum balance clause resulted in retaining higher credit balances which resulted in higher ARPU figures. SMS GupShup and Vodafone plan to roll this activity across another 25 network circles in due course of time.
